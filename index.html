Foothold of Growth

MiniLD #63: Fusion (Platformer + Business Simulator)

Control: W,A,S,D (or Arrow) to move. Space to jump. Up/Down to talk people.

How to Play:
Talk to the Assistant to hire workers.
Upgrade the products and train the workers.
Watch the demands and monthly cost.
Avoid hazards (fire, lightning, and crocs).
Get extra money and food.
Don't die.

Source code: https://github.com/euske/bizplat
